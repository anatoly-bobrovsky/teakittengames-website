About the Studio

Tea Kitten Games is an independent game studio crafting cozy, wholesome games for the soul. We believe games can be digital sanctuaries — places where warmth meets wonder, and every player feels at home.

Our Games

Chaos Inside Me

In Development 🔮

Point & Click · Mystery · Psychology

A cozy point & click puzzle where you act as a psychotherapist tidying up the "internal houses" of your patients. Rearrange artifacts of memory to turn trauma into peace.

  • ✦ Metaphorical healing mechanics — your actions are direct metaphors for the therapeutic process
  • ✦ Unique patient stories with layered psychological depth
  • ✦ Gentle, non-judgmental atmosphere

Fluffy Workshop

In Development 🔮

Clicker · Cozy · Crafting

A meditative clicker game where grooming is just the beginning. Shear adorable animals, collect their wool, and craft unique toys in your cozy studio.

  • ✦ Meditative shearing mechanics with rewarding progression
  • ✦ Toy crafting system — turn simple wool into magical pom-pom toys
  • ✦ No timers, no penalties, no wrong way to play
